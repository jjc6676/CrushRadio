# Product Requirements: CrushRadio

## Purpose
CrushRadio aims to provide a modern, user-friendly platform for streaming online radio and YouTube audio, with a focus on discoverability, ease of use, and extensibility.

## Core Features
- Play and manage online radio streams.
- Search and play YouTube audio.
- Responsive, intuitive UI.
- Modular architecture for easy feature expansion.

## User Stories
- As a user, I want to search for and play radio stations.
- As a user, I want to search for and play YouTube audio.
- As a user, I want a clean, responsive interface.
- As a developer, I want to easily add new integrations and features.

## Non-Functional Requirements
- Fast load times and responsive interactions.
- Robust error handling.
- Scalable codebase for future growth.

## Future Enhancements
- User authentication and personalization.
- Playlists and favorites management.
- Social sharing and recommendations.
